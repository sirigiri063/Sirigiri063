---

**🏆 Business Impact**

- Reduced executive reporting time by consolidating multiple Excel sheets into a single interactive dashboard
- Enabled faster identification of underperforming segments, supporting proactive resource reallocation
- Provided a reusable reporting template scalable to new periods without manual rebuilding

**🧰 Technology Stack**

| Layer | Tools Used |
|:---|:---|
| Data Source | Retail transaction data — sales, customer, and product records |
| Data Preparation | Microsoft Excel — cleaning, type validation, outlier handling |
| Query & Transformation | PostgreSQL — segmentation queries, aggregations, cohort preparation |
| Visualisation | Microsoft Power BI — dual-dashboard architecture, DAX, bookmarks |

---

**📏 Scale & Scope**

- Multi-year retail transaction dataset covering thousands of customer records
- Segmented across demographic dimensions: gender, age group, product category, and purchase timing
- Dual-dashboard structure enabling distinct analysis for sales teams vs marketing teams

---

**📊 Dashboard Features**

| Dashboard | Purpose |
|:---|:---|
| Sales Performance Dashboard | Revenue by period, category contribution, top SKUs, regional breakdown |
| Customer Insights Dashboard | Age and gender segmentation, purchase timing heatmaps, customer lifetime value indicators |

---

**👤 Customer Insights Uncovered**

- The **45–54 age group** generated the highest revenue but was underrepresented in marketing-facing metrics — creating a targeting gap
- Female customers showed higher average basket sizes in specific categories compared to male segments
- Weekend purchasing spikes identified across specific product lines — supporting promotional timing strategy
- Repeat purchase frequency varied significantly across age cohorts — informing loyalty programme prioritisation

---

**🏆 Business Impact**

- Enabled the marketing team to realign campaign spend toward the highest-value, under-targeted demographic
- Provided sales leadership with a clear view of category performance without reliance on manual Excel reports
